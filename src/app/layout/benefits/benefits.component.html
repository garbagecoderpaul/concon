Film Productions

You will work closely with the filming crew, and have the opportunity to vote on and influence the direction of the documentary film including the filming locations, interviewees, and subplots. Additionally, you will be involved in the day-to-day activities and creative aspects at all stages of the production.

Comic books

You'll be able to see early artwork and follow the comic book production process. Through your ability to vote, you can participate in the production process by providing input on storyline direction, character design, and art.

Events & Promotions

As a member, you'll get exclusive invites to events and promotions, along with early access to our merchandise.

Film streaming Revenue

Holders of NFTs will own the intellectual property for the film and be entitled to participate in the profits generated by streaming the documentary.
