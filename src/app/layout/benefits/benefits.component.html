Inclusive Consensus

The project success depends on understanding global tensions by including diverse viewpoints across demographics. The voices of different nationalities, political orientations, religions, ages, and underrepresented groups are essential. Not including these would result in an incomplete portrayal, affecting scenario modeling and future outcomes.

In partnership with Public Choice theory experts, we're developing ways to include these diverse voices in our consensus-building process. Embracing pluralism is not an ideological choice but a pragmatic imperative.

Consensus Games

We gather diverse individuals through organizational games to discuss global issues, such as geopolitical tensions, religious-ontological conflicts, and socio-economic disparities. Participants collaboratively map scenarios, highlighting the interaction of various forces to uncover overlooked insights. These games form the basis of an interactive documentary, immersively exploring these issues. We anticipate this approach may lead to innovative solutions to complex global challenges.
