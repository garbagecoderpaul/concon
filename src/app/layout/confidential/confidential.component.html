The voting mechanism

The voting mechanism for the documentary film and comic is based on the ownership of NFTs. Each regular NFT represents one vote, while a rare NFT represents 100 votes and a unique NFT represents 500 votes.

  • regular NFT = 1 vote
  • rare NFT = 100 votes
  • unique NFT = 500 votes

To participate in the voting process, community members must own at least one NFT. The more NFTs they own, the more voting power they have. The community members will submit their proposals and ideas for the project, and these ideas will be put up for a vote. The voting process will be conducted in a transparent and fair manner. Each community member can cast their vote by connecting their wallet with the UC NFTs to the website and submitting the form. The total number of votes will be calculated by adding up the voting power of all UC NFTs in the wallet. Once the voting period is over, the results will be tallied, and the winning proposals will be implemented in the documentary or comic. Note, that rare and unique NFTs carry significantly more voting power than regular NFTs.
