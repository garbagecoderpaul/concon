From Spectator to Director

The web3 project is a unique and innovative approach to creating a documentary about the Russian Ukrainian war. The project aims to show the conflict from different angles and scopes, highlighting how decisions made by major players such as politicians, oligarchs, and media affect ordinary people caught up in the conflict. The documentary will be based on real facts and people involved in the conflict, providing an authentic and compelling look at the war.

What makes this project truly unique is its integration of web3 technology. By using NFTs and allowing NFT holders to vote on the direction of the film production, the project creates a collaborative and community-driven approach to documentary making. NFT holders will have a say in crucial decisions such as where to go for interviews, providing a level of engagement and investment in the project not seen in traditional documentary making. This approach has the potential to create a truly immersive and engaging documentary experience, one that is shaped by the collective decision-making of a community of stakeholders.

About our Filming Crew

Filmmakers & Investigative Journalists

Our passionate filmmaking team is composed of professional, award-winning documentary filmmakers and investigative journalists with a strong commitment to truth-telling.

Our team includes seasoned journalists with over 20 years of experience. We unite people from different countries who share a common passion for uncovering the facts surrounding the Russian-Ukrainian conflict.

With a focus on seeking the truth and presenting it to the world, we bring a fresh perspective to this complex and often-misunderstood topic.

Our team is dedicated to producing high-quality film that will educate, inform, and inspire audiences worldwide.
