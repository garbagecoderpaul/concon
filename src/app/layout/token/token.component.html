CON Token

In the wake of global upheaval, we welcome you at the Concordia enclave born from the ashes of a world reshaped by cataclysmic events. Our token, CON, embodies resilience amidst a transformed landscape where conventional norms shattered. As the financial edifice crumbled, crypto emerged as the currency of survival, liberating us from the shackles of defunct central systems.

Governments are fading into history, paving the way for decentralized, grassroots-driven societies. The scars of devastating conflicts, orchestrated by crumbling regimes, echo across Europe, the Middle East, Central America, and South Eastern Asia. In their wake, a groundswell of disillusionment and revolt emerged, fostering a yearning for a new order.

Amidst this chaos, a beacon of readiness emerged—crypto enthusiasts and preppers who foresaw the storm brewing on the horizon. Their preparedness, both in digital assets and fortified shelters, became the pillars of survival.

Retroactive Public Goods Funding is based on the idea that it’s easier to agree on what *was* useful in the past than what *might* be useful in the future. The first round of Retro Funding will finance a series of games, culminating in the production of a documentary film.

About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to lead a decentralized movement, uniting those weary of wars and corrupt governments with those preparing for change. Our ecosystem fosters collaboration, nurturing collective wisdom. Embracing individual freedom, we unite to shape our shared destiny, based on agreements and interest rather than traditional confines of fears and ideologies. Join us in crafting a new, collaborative future.

Our Vision

We envision a future where there are decentralized authorities rulling the order. A world where individual voices harmonize into a chorus of collective choice. Here, freedom flourishes, allowing individuals to worship and love without constraint, embracing dialogue as their guiding principle. Communities thrive and bound not by force but by shared interests, forging agreements that reflect their collective will. This is a realm of shared understanding, where differences foster consensus for the orchestre of diverse. It's a world where the symphony of shared interests orchestrates a harmonious future for all.

Concordia House

We thrive on the power of community engagement. The Concordia House members participate in both the games and the creative aspects of the film. The members evaluate the project's progress and the value it has delivered. As the project continues to progress and deliver value, both the Concordia House and Concordia Foundation adjust the price floor upwards. This adjustment enables further funding rounds by buying back tokens at higher prices. This ongoing process motivates the team of contributors to persist in developing the project and delivering value.
